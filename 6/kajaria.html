About Company

Kajaria

The largest manufacturer of ceramic and vitrified tiles in India with an annual aggregate capacity of 73 mn. sq. meters, distributed across eight plants. Equipped with cutting-edge technology, we at Kajaria strive to deliver quality products and services to our customers. Our team of experts work around the clock to ensure that the quality of our product is never compromised.In order to cater to the specific needs of our customers we produce various categories of tiles, including kitchen tiles, bathroom tiles,wall tiles, floor tiles and exterior wall tiles. We aim at producing tiles that complement the distinctive style of our customers and cater to the vast needs of Indian audience. All of our tiles are crafted with intense care using state-of-the-art technology to ensure that we live up to the expectations of our buyers. Choicest of materials are used to produce our magnificent range of products.
