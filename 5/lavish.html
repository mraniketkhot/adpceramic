Double charge Tiles

Double charge vitrified tiles are fed through a press that prints the pattern with a double layer of pigment, 3 to 4 mm thicker than other types of tile. This process does not permit complex patterns but results in a long-wearing tile surface, suitable for heavy traffic commercial projects

Wooden Tiles

Unlike hardwood floors, wood-look tiles are much durable to scratches, spills, and dirt. So once you install these tiles, they will look good for years to come. Wood tiles can hold water & moisture and so are very easy to maintain. Wood tiles are quite versatile

Full Body Tiles

Full body vitrified tiles also known as Rectified Tiles are formed by mixing clay, feldspar, quartz, and silica at high temperatures. During this process, grains of the required color get added to the base tile, which creates a homogenous color that runs throughout the tile.

Soluble Salt Tiles

Soluble Salt tiles are ivory-based tiles (plain ivory and design ivory) that can be developed using simple technology. The design on the surface of the tile is printed using liquid color screen printing. The tile material absorbs the soluble salt to paint the design on the tile after it is heated in the kiln.
